The following is a complete list of powerups active on the current server:

Powerup Description
Become Sentry You will transform into a Level 2 Sentry upon using this powerup, but be wary of sappers. Jumping or attacking will return control to you.
Blast Upon use, Blast creates an explosion at your location, dealing damage to enemies and enemy buildings. Combine with other damage sources for quicker kills.
Catapult Catapult launches you at a slight upward angle in the approximate direction you're looking. Use this powerup to quickly drop into where your enemies are positioned.
Dizzy Bomb The Dizzy Bomb powerup will affect nearby enemies, causing their screens to sway from side to side and impeding their movement for a short period of time.
Effect Burst Effect Burst causes nearby enemies to either bleed, combust, soak in jarate or soak in milk, randomly per afflicted player, and all for 10 seconds.
Frost Touch Using Frost Touch allows you to turn enemies you touch into ice statues for 3 seconds. The powerup itself lasts 8 seconds, so make good use of it.
Ghost You become a ghost for 5 seconds and stun any enemy near you for 2 seconds after using this powerup. Can also be used to float to unreachable places.
Gyrocopter The Gyrocopter powerup lowers your gravity to 25% for 5 seconds. This can be used to clear large gaps or reach new heights, if you are decent at parkour.
Magnetism For 5 seconds after using Magnetism, enemies will be repelled from you, unless you hold out your melee weapon, then enemies will be brought towards you.
Mega Mann You become giant for 10 seconds after using Mega Mann, overhealed 4 times your original health, up to 4 times your maximum health, 3 times if you are a Heavy.
Shock Absorber Shock Absorber allows you to resist 75% of all damage and take no knockback for 5 seconds. Use this when trying to kill a player with a high push force.
Super Bounce Super Bounce reverses your fall speed when you touch the ground and you take no fall damage for 5 seconds. Use it from great heights to surprise your enemies.
Super Jump Super Jump launches you into the air. If you jump before using this powerup, you will travel even higher, just watch out for fall damage.
Super Speed The Super Speed powerup drastically speeds up your movement, but wears off over time. It's great for dodging focused fire.
Teleportation Telelporation warps you to a random Engineer's active exit Teleporter on your team. If there aren't any, you will be respawned instead.
Time Travel Time Travel makes you invisible and fast for 3 seconds, but you cannot attack. Use this to your advantage to get past sentries and difficult opponents.
Mystery Mystery is a completely randomised powerup. It can take on the effects of any other powerup when used. Are you feeling lucky?
ULTRA POWERUP!! This very rare powerup will grant you Frost Touch, Magnetism, Shock Absorber, critical hits and Mega Mann health all at once and more, all for 10 seconds!
