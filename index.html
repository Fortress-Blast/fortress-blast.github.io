Introduction Powerups Credits

Fortress Blast adds collectable powerups to a map that give special abilities for a short amount of time. If you have a powerup, you will be able to see what it is in the top-right corner of your screen. Press your 'Special attack' key to use a powerup. You can set this in TF2's Options. Check out the Powerups submenu for information on each collectible.

Powerup Description
Gyrocopter The Gyrocopter powerup lowers your gravity to 25%. This powerup can be used to clear large gaps or reach new heights, if you are decent at parkour.
Shock Absorber Shock Absorber allows you to resist 75% of all damage and not take knockback. Use this when trying take down a player with a high push force.
