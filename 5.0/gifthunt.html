Gift Hunt is an exclusive mini game mode that runs inside Fortress Blast and supports a wide variety of map game modes including Capture the Flag, Control Points, King of the Hill and Payload Race.

In this mini game mode, small gifts appear randomly around the map and the map's objectives start off locked. It's your job to collect the required number of gifts and then complete the objective before the other team. Only once all the gifts on the map have been collected will more appear.

On one-sided maps, such as Attack/Defense and Payload, gifts do not spawn until setup time is over and the round timer starts paused. If the BLU team collects the required number of gifts, the objective will unlock as normal, but if the RED team does so, the round timer will start ticking down.

Not all servers run Gift Hunt- it is disabled by default.
