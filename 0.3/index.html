Fortress Blast adds collectable powerups to a map that give special abilities for a short amount of time. If you have a powerup, you will be able to see what it is in the top-right corner of your screen. Press your 'Special attack' key to use a powerup. You can set this in TF2's Options. Check out the Powerups submenu for information on each collectible.

  • Bots can pick up powerups and randomly use them based on a user-defined range of time.
  • By default, Mannpower powerups are replaced with Fortress Blast powerups.
Powerup Description

Blast		 Upon use, this powerup creates an explosion at your location, dealing higher damage to closer enemies. It is most effective when combined with other sources of damage.

Gyrocopter		 The Gyrocopter powerup lowers your gravity to 25%. This powerup can be used to clear large gaps or reach new heights, if you are decent at parkour.

Shock Absorber		 Shock Absorber allows you to resist 75% of all damage and not take knockback. Use this when trying take down a player with a high push force.

Super Bounce		 While this powerup is active, you are forced to uncontrollably bunny hop. This is mainly used to clear gaps by bouncing but you can also trick players with your unpredictable movement.

Super Jump		 Plain and simple, Super Jump launches you into the air. If you jump before using this powerup, you will travel even higher, just watch out for fall damage.

Super Speed		 The Super Speed powerup drastically speeds up your movement, but wears off over time. It's great for dodging focused fire.

Time Travel		 Using this powerup makes you invisible and fast, but prevents you from attacking. Use this to your advantage in order to get past sentries or difficult opponents.
