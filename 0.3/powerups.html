The following is a complete list of powerups available in this version of Fortress Blast:

Powerup Description

Blast		 Upon use, Blast creates an explosion at your location, dealing higher damage to closer enemies. Combine with other sources of damage for quicker kills.

Gyrocopter		 The Gyrocopter powerup lowers your gravity to 25%. This powerup can be used to clear large gaps or reach new heights, if you are decent at parkour.

Shock Absorber		 Shock Absorber allows you to resist 75% of all damage and take no knockback. Use this when trying take down a player with a high push force.

Super Bounce		 While Super Bounce is active, your fall speed is reversed when you touch the ground. Use it from great heights to surprise your enemies.

Super Jump		 Super Jump launches you into the air. If you jump before using this powerup, you will travel even higher, just watch out for fall damage.

Super Speed		 The Super Speed powerup drastically speeds up your movement, but wears off over time. It's great for dodging focused fire.

Time Travel		 Using Time Travel makes you invisible and fast, but you cannot attack. Use this to your advantage in order to get past sentries or difficult opponents.
