If the current server has maps that start with 'gh_', you can play Fortress Blast's exclusive mini-gamemode: Gift Hunt.

In Gift Hunt, small gifts appear in random batches around the map, and it's your job to collect the required number of gifts and complete the map's objective before the other team.

Only once an entire batch of gifts has been cleared will another appear. Gifts stop spawning once both teams have collected the required amount.

Just like Fortress Blast, the Gift Hunt gamemode is freely available for mappers to implement into their own projects. You can create your own Gift Hunt map by using the prefab located at https://gamebanana.com/somethingsomething.
