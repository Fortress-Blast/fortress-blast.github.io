If the current server has maps that start with 'gh_', you can play Fortress Blast's exclusive mini-gamemode: Gift Hunt.

In Gift Hunt, your job is to collect the required number of gifts, then complete the map's objective before the other team.

Gifts spawn in random batches around the map, and only once an entire batch has been cleared will another activate. Gifts continue to spawn even when one team has collected the required amount.
